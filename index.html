Baking Sweet Memories Since 2014

At Monstera Bakery, our recipe is simple: a cake should not only look like a piece of art but taste like a slice of heaven. Founded by artisan pastry designer Clara Winters, we blend classic European technique with fresh, local Pacific Northwest flavors.

We believe every event deserves a showstopping dessert. That's why every petal is hand-piped, every tier is baked from scratch, and every design is entirely unique to you.
